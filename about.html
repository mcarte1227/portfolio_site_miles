ABOUT ME

I’m an aspiring freelance UX/UI designer based in the Metro Atlanta area. As well as designing, I also have a passion for video games, despite not having the time to sit down and play any as of late. I especially love games when it comes down to their visual design and storytelling. I also love experiencing any new fantasy / sci-fi story that piques my interest, and would love to possibly one day help design the UI for a video game. I studied digital animation in university before deciding to make the switch to learn about UX and UI design, so I’m quite skilled in drawing, illustration, and 2D animation to some degree. I look forward to utilizing the skills I have learned into products that will serve as both aesthetically pleasing as well as providing the best functional experience!
