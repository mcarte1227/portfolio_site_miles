ABOUT ME

I’m an aspiring freelance UX/UI designer based in the Metro Atlanta area. As well as designing, I also love video games, especially when it comes to their visual design and aesthetics. My favorite genres include RPG’s, fighting and action games. I also love experiencing any new fantasy / sci-fi story that piques my interest, and would love to one day help design the UI for a video game. I studied digital animation in university before deciding to make the switch to learn about UX and UI design, so I’m quite skilled in drawing, illustration, and 2D animation to some degree. I look forward to utilizing the skills I have learned into products that will serve as both aesthetically pleasing and provides the best functional experience!
